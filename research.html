Research Areas

1. Beam Physics & Particle Accelerators

Our group investigates high-brightness electron beams, beam transport, space-charge dominated regimes, and precision magnetic focusing systems.

2. RF & DC Electron Sources

We study thermionic, gridded, and RF-driven cathode systems aimed at producing low-emittance electron beams for accelerator applications.

3. Computational Electromagnetics

  • Finite element modelling (COMSOL, CST Studio Suite)
  • Custom electromagnetic solvers (Poisson solvers, magnetostatic fields)
  • Particle tracking and PIC simulations

4. Superconducting & Normal Conducting Magnets

Design and optimization of high-field solenoids and focusing magnets for accelerator beamlines and diagnostic systems.

5. High-power RF Devices

Study of RF generation, electron bunching, and beam-wave interactions in vacuum electronic devices.
